# Roadmap

Planned features for Livery AI Studio.

---

- [ ] **Sponsor placement** — apply sponsor logos to a generated livery; upload logo images, position and scale them on the car, then bake down to a final deployable texture

- [ ] **Specular map generation** — generate matching specular maps from a livery (R = metallic, G = roughness, B = clearcoat) and deploy them alongside the car texture

- [ ] **Helmet & race suit painting** — extend AI generation to helmets and race suits, not just the car body
